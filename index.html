Welcome!

Hi! My name is Dani Vykydal. I'm an undergraduate student at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor where I study UX Design at the School of Information.

One of my greatest passions is to travel. I've had the privilege of visiting many different places growing up. Some of the trips I took were with family, while others were with friends or school groups.

This website details my adventures, as well as provides some tips I've learned from them. My hope is that this site will inspire you to go on your own travels.
