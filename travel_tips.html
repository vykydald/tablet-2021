Travel Tips

Planning

  • -- Prepare several weeks in advance
  • -- Use a travel book to give you ideas on where you want to go
  • -- Book flights early for cheaper prices

Packing

  • -- Use a packing list
  • -- Use packing cubes or roll your clothes to organize and save space
  • -- Don't bring a lot of hair products with you
  • -- Test the weight of your bags
  • -- Make sure to bring a universal adapter if you are traveling out of the country

Sightseeing

  • -- Eat local food
  • -- Bring a camera to take pictures
  • -- Wake up early to make the most of your day and avoid crowds
  • -- Be respectful of different cultures
  • -- Don't be afraid to try new things

Safety

  • -- Travel with those you trust
  • -- Inform someone of your travel plans
  • -- Don't put all of your money in one bag
